Important Voter Information for North Carolina

Voter ID Requirements

Photo ID is required to vote in North Carolina, with exceptions for reasonable impediment.

Registration Deadline

You must register to vote at least 25 days before an election.

Early Voting

Early voting begins 17 days before Election Day and ends 3 days before.

Absentee Voting

Absentee voting is available for qualifying reasons or for all voters 65 and older.
