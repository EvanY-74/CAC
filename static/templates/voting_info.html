Photo ID Requirement

North Carolina requires photo ID to vote. Acceptable forms include:

  • NC driver's license
  • NC DMV-issued ID card
  • U.S. passport
  • U.S. military ID
  • NC voter photo ID card (free from DMV)

Can't get ID? You may still vote by signing a reasonable impediment declaration.

Election Day Voting

Vote at your assigned polling place on Election Day from 6:30 AM to 7:30 PM.

Early Voting

Vote early at any early voting site in your county during the 17-day early voting period.

Absentee Voting

Vote by mail if you're 65+, have a qualifying reason, or are a military/overseas voter.

Same-Day Registration

North Carolina allows same-day voter registration during the early voting period. You can register and vote at the same time at any early voting site.

Not Registered Yet?

You can register to vote:

  • Online at ncsbe.gov
  • By mail using a voter registration form
  • In person at your county board of elections
  • At the DMV when getting/renewing your license
  • During early voting (same-day registration)
