About Your Representatives

Your representatives work at different levels of government:

  • Federal: President, U.S. Senators, U.S. House Representative
  • State: Governor, State Senators, State House Representatives
  • Local: Mayor, City Council, County Commissioners, School Board
Contact Your Representatives

Ways to reach out to your representatives:

  • Call their offices (most effective)
  • Send emails through official websites
  • Write letters to their office addresses
  • Attend town halls and public meetings
  • Follow them on social media
Stay Engaged

Keep up with your representatives:

  • Read their position statements
  • Sign up for their newsletters
  • Attend community events
  • Vote in all elections they appear on
